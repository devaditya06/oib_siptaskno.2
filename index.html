Steve Jobs

A Visionary, Innovator, and Pioneer

Biography

Steve Jobs (1955-2011) was an American business magnate, industrial designer, investor, and media proprietor. He was the chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), and co-founder of Apple Inc., chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar, a member of The Walt Disney Company's board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar, and the founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.

His impact on technology and design is immeasurable, and his vision for the future changed the way we live our lives. From the Apple II and Macintosh to the iPod, iPhone, and iPad, Jobs' innovations continue to shape the world today.
